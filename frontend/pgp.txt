Replace this file with your own PGP public key.

Generate one:
  gpg --full-generate-key          # RSA 4096 or Ed25519
  gpg --armor --export YOUR_KEY_ID > frontend/pgp.txt

Publish the fingerprint in frontend/terms.html and frontend/docs.html
where <YOUR-PGP-FINGERPRINT> appears.

The PGP key is how users send encrypted abuse reports and contact the operator
without creating plaintext logs.
